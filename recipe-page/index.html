Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with salt and pepper until well mixed.
  2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil.
  3. Cook the omelette: Pour in the eggs and tilt the pan to spread them evenly.
  4. Add fillings (optional): When the eggs begin to set, sprinkle your chosen fillings.
  5. Fold and serve: Carefully fold one side over the fillings and cook for another minute.
  6. Enjoy: Serve hot with additional salt and pepper if needed.
